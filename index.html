About CP2 Project

The CP2 project enhances cultural exchange and collaborative production among Asia-Pacific countries by providing equipment, training, and an online platform for producing and broadcasting cultural content.

Originally designed for physical training and production, the project successfully evolved to virtual collaboration during the COVID-19 pandemic, supporting APAN meetings and enabling wider participation in digital performances, live streaming, and content archiving across the TEIN network.

Our Mission

To develop multimedia and cyber performance capabilities across Asia-Pacific NRENs using collaborative production, streaming, and digital archiving tools for long-term cultural exchange and documentation.
